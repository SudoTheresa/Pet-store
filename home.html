What is DreamPets?

DreamPets is an association which was initially started in 1962 by Lady Winterbottom and her dear son Frederick Winterbottom.
The aim of the organisation was to provide the best service and quality pet products to our clients.
We are proud to say that the legacy has been carried on for decades.
DreamPets is now a well known and trusted pet products supplier by millions of people.
Most of our food products are naturally made thus you don't have to worry about any unhealthy chemicals in your pets food.
Our team is looking forward to providing you with all the care, products and advice to help keep your Pets in top condition, healthy and happy.
