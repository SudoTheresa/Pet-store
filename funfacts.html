How to take care of your pet

Cats

1.Always cuddle with your cat. Cats love plenty of human companionship.

2.Provide a clean and comfortable bed. We have one of the best pet beds in the industry. Check them out.

3.Always trim your cats nails. Grooming is essential and should be done frequently

4.Take your dog for a cat everyday. Your cat also needs some exercise just like you do

5.Train your cat with simple commands.

6.Feed them quality food and do not overfeed or underfeed. Always serve food to them nicely.

Dogs

1.Always serve them with fresh water. It is good for the dogs metabolism

2.Practise good dental care and hygiene. A clean dog is a happy dog

3.Always trim your dogs nails. They might bite but its okay.

4.Take your dog for a walk everyday. Dogs love walking, you should do it regularly.

5.Train your dog with simple commands. They understand better and quicker

6.Feed them quality food and do not overfeed or underfeed.
